# Governance & Operations Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the governance structures, federation model, tier operations, and phase evolution for the Radium ecosystem.

## Overview

The governance and operations framework enables:

- **DAO Structure**: Decentralized governance for the ecosystem
- **Federation Model**: Multi-organization collaboration
- **Tier Operations**: Different service levels and access models
- **Phase Evolution**: Structured growth and development phases
